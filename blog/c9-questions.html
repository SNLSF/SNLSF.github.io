Asking Good Questions
It's in your best interest.
02.22.2015.

I was surprised how quickly I caught on to asking good questions in the Google+ community we utilized during Phase 0. Asking good questions has a purpose way beyond being curteous to your audience- it is completely in your self interest to do it if you ever want to get unstuck on your problem. Don't have a lot of time to kill waiting for an answer? Great- then be specific, include error messages, copy/paste your solution directly from your worksheet, and write a specific title/intro to your question.

I found the best way for me to get a clear answer was to post a question as concise and clear as possible. Including the link to my Github for longer code proved effective, especially since I could comment within the doc where I thought the problem was going wrong (or previous attempts).
